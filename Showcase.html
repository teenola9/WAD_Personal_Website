Showcase

A selection of academic and professional projects demonstrating problem solving, design, and leadership.

UI / UX Design

My first ever UI/UX assignment required me to build an interface that matched the client’s needs precisely. Initially, it was challenging because I had no prior experience in this field, understanding accessibility rules, or layout psychology.

After researching design patterns from competitors and experimenting with prototypes, I created a user friendly interface that met all criteria and secured a high grade. This project strengthened my attention to detail and introduced me to design systems and wireframing.

Group University Presentation

As part of a team assignment, we were tasked with analysing a university problem and presenting a full solution using AI & Data. Throughout the project, I contributed heavily to the research, visual design, and slide structure.

A challenge we faced was uneven workload distribution among group members. I had to step up as other team members were not able to ensure the deadlines were met. The result was a successful, well-delivered presentation graded by university lecturers.

Palazzo 22 – Digital Transformation

This project involved redesigning the digital identity of an Italian restaurant. I implemented interactive maps, a booking system, menu structure, and customer centric components to improve user experience.

The challenge was creating a visually vibrant but professional experience that remained readable over a GIF background. Careful contrast, spacing, and animation timing delivered a seamless feel.

P22 Design Philosophy

The structure of this showcase page follows a luxury approach inspired by local restaurants' such as Cut & Craft in order to gain a competitive edge. It features an elevated box with soft shadows, wide spacing, and smooth hover animations.

Animations are intentionally amplified when hovered over: others rise up slightly, some change colour, drawing the user's attention to each piece. The centre alignment and large typography ensure strong readability and create a an easy digital experience consistent with the rest of my website.
