Before You Contact Me

To help me provide you with the most accurate and helpful response, please consider including the following information in your initial message:

  • Project Overview: A brief description of what you're looking to build or improve
  • Timeline: When you need the project completed
  • Budget Range: An approximate budget helps ensure we're a good fit
  • Current Status: Whether you have existing materials (design files, content, domain, etc.)
  • Specific Requirements: Any particular features or functionality you need

Don't worry if you don't have all these details yet - I'm happy to have an exploratory conversation to help you clarify your needs and determine the best approach for your project.
