Business Challenge: "La Bella Cucina," a traditional family-owned Italian restaurant, recognized the need to modernize their customer engagement strategy. Despite serving excellent food and having loyal local customers, they were missing opportunities to reach younger demographics and tourists who research restaurants online before visiting.

Project Goals:

  • Improve Customer Engagement: Create digital touchpoints where customers can interact with the restaurant beyond phone calls
  • Modernize Communication: Establish presence on social media platforms where potential customers are active
  • Streamline Bookings: Provide an easy-to-use online booking request system that reduces phone call volume
  • Gather Feedback: Create a structured channel for customer feedback and inquiries
  • Build Online Presence: Establish credibility with an attractive, functional website that showcases the restaurant

Solution: I developed an interactive web page that serves as the restaurant's digital hub, featuring integrated social media links, contact information, and an automated booking/feedback form system.

Design Concept & Technical Implementation

User-Centered Design Philosophy

The design prioritizes simplicity and immediate action. Research shows that users make decisions about restaurant bookings within seconds, so the page needed to be intuitive and trustworthy from first glance.

Structural Decisions

  • Single-Page Layout: Everything is accessible without navigation, reducing friction and cognitive load
  • Progressive Disclosure: Information is presented in logical order: contact info ‚Üí social proof ‚Üí booking form
  • Visual Hierarchy: Most important elements (contact details, booking form) use larger fonts and prominent positioning
  • Consistent Spacing: Generous whitespace between sections prevents overwhelming the user

Technical Implementation

The interactive form uses JavaScript to validate user input and generate a properly formatted email with all booking details. The mailto: protocol opens the user's default email client with pre-filled subject and body text, ensuring the restaurant receives structured, consistent information.

Form validation prevents submission of incomplete data, while clear error messages guide users to correct any issues. The responsive design uses CSS Grid and Flexbox to adapt seamlessly across device sizes.

Color and Typography Choices

The color scheme uses warm, inviting tones that evoke Italian cuisine and family dining. Purple and blue accents provide a modern touch while maintaining professional credibility. Typography is clean and highly readable, with appropriate font sizes for different viewport sizes.

Accessibility Considerations

  • All form fields have clear labels and required field indicators
  • Color contrast ratios meet WCAG accessibility standards
  • Form inputs include helpful placeholder text
  • Links clearly indicate their purpose and destination
  • The design works with keyboard navigation
